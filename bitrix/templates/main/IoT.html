HYPERCONNECTION THROUGH IoT

Consumer Engagement

Itransition helps businesses explore and develop connected products and services to better win, serve, and retain customers. Offering more engagement and advantage.

Our custom IoT solutions raise brand awareness and provide new opportunities to develop cognitive, emotional and behavioral metrics and a customer-centered approach.

Financial Transactions

We enable payments on connected devices, thus, effectively increasing the number of payment endpoints. Itransition integrates near field communication (NFC) and embedded secure element (eSE) solutions to deliver secure payment processing systems that leverage connectivity to conveniently process credit and debit transactions around the globe.

IoT DATA ANALYTICS

Create actionable business intelligence and obtain meaningful insights by analyzing data generated by devices connected to an IoT platform.

Our team crafts custom systems and cloud infrastructures that ingest and analyze realtime and contextual information generated from IoT sensors and gadgets and enable decision makers to run batch operations on accumulated data at scheduled time periods and make predictions based on statistical and machine learning techniques.

Connected Retail Experiences

Spice your sales with IoT devices and connected objects with innovative use cases.

Itransition develops ecosystems that connect physical and digital sales, allowing bidirectional, real-time interaction with your consumers both inside and outside the store, delivering personalized content, and enhancing user experiences through context-based interaction and NFC-enabled payments.

Shared Commerce Solutions

Take your brick-and-mortar operations online to grow your business exponentially.

Our shared commerce solutions remove tedious and complicated tasks of consolidating data between offline and online sales, and bridge the gap by synchronizing and streamlining financial and product information across all sales channels, whilst providing you with quick and easy access to real-time customer insights.

PROTOTYPING WITH IoT BOARDS & PLATFORMS

Boards

Itransition utilizes third-party microcontroller-based boards, System on Chip (SOC) boards, Single-board Computers (SBC) and purpose-built boards with Wi-Fi, cellular and Ethernet interfaces to quickly build IoT prototypes with a cloud-based backend as a secure gateway to interact with the device through a modern API and develop web services around it.

Platforms

We leverage third-party pre-configured platforms to accelerate IoT projects. Using open and flexible solutions, we connect a broad range of existing devices and gadgets or prototypes through these platforms and integrate them with your existing business systems to help you make the best use of the data and processes you already have, whilst keeping the TCO at bay.
